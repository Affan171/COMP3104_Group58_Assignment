The George Brown College of Applied Arts and Technology is a public, fully accredited college 
of applied arts and technology. 

It has three campuses in downtown Toronto (Ontario, Canada) which are Casaloma, St. James 
and Waterfront. 

Like many other colleges in Ontario, George Brown College was chartered in 1966 by the government 
of Ontario and opened the next year.

George Brown offers more than 170 full-time programs in art and design, business, 
community services, early childhood education, construction and engineering technologies, 
health sciences, hospitality and culinary arts, preparatory studies, as well as specialized 
programs and services for recent immigrants and international students.
